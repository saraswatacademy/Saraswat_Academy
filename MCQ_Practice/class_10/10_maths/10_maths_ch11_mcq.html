Q1. The circumference of a circle of radius 7 cm is

(a) 22 cm
(b) 44 cm
(c) 49 cm
(d) 154 cm

Answer: (b) 44 cm

Solution: Circumference = 2πr = 2 × 22/7 × 7 = 44 cm.

Q2. The area of a circle of radius 7 cm is

(a) 154 cm²
(b) 144 cm²
(c) 49 cm²
(d) 308 cm²

Answer: (a) 154 cm²

Solution: Area = πr² = 22/7 × 7 × 7 = 154 cm².

Q3. The area of a semicircle is

(a) πr²

(c) ½πr²
(d) ¼πr²

Answer: (c) ½πr²

Q5. The length of an arc of 90° in a circle of radius 14 cm is

(a) 22 cm

(c) 11 cm

Solution: Arc Length = (90/360) × 2πr = 22 cm.
