50 Board-Level MCQs with Answers and Solutions

Q1. Probability of an impossible event is

(a) 0
(b) 1
(c) 1/2
(d) 2

Answer: (a) 0

Q2. Probability of a sure event is

(a) 0
(b) 1/2
(c) 1
(d) 2

Answer: (c) 1

Q3. Probability always lies between

(a) -1 and 1
(b) 0 and 1
(c) 1 and 2
(d) -2 and 2

Answer: (b) 0 and 1

Q4. A coin is tossed once. Probability of getting a Head is

(a) 0
(b) 1/4
(c) 1/2
(d) 1

Answer: (c) 1/2

Q5. A die is thrown once. Probability of getting 6 is

(a) 1/3
(b) 1/6
(c) 1/2
(d) 1

(a) 1/2

(c) 1/6

Q8. Probability of getting a prime number on a die is

(a) 1/6
(b) 1/3
(c) 1/2
(d) 2/3

Answer: (c) 1/2

Solution: Prime numbers are 2, 3, 5. Probability = 3/6 = 1/2.

Q50. Which statement is true?

(a) Probability can be negative.
(b) Probability can be greater than 1.
(c) Probability lies between 0 and 1 inclusive.
(d) Probability is always equal to 1.

Answer: (c) Probability lies between 0 and 1 inclusive.

Solution: For any event E, 0 ≤ P(E) ≤ 1.
