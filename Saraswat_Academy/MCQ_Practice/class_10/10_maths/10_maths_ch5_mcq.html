Q1. Which of the following is an Arithmetic Progression (A.P.)?

(a) 2, 4, 8, 16
(b) 3, 7, 11, 15
(c) 1, 4, 9, 16
(d) 2, 6, 18, 54

Answer: (b) 3, 7, 11, 15

Solution: The common difference is 4.

Q2. The common difference of 5, 9, 13, 17, ... is

Q4. Find the 10th term of the A.P. 2, 5, 8, 11, ...

(a) 26
(b) 27
(c) 29
(d) 30

Answer: (c) 29

Solution: a = 2, d = 3.
a₁₀ = a + 9d = 2 + 27 = 29.

Q7. Which term of the A.P. 3, 8, 13, 18, ... is 63?
