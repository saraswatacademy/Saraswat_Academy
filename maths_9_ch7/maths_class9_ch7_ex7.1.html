Class 9 - Chapter 7: The mathematics of maybe: Introduction to Probability

NCERTChapter 7Solution- Exercises Set 7.1

Q1: Rank the following events on a scale from 0 (Impossible) to 1 (Certain).

(i) The next Monday will come after Sunday.

Ranking: 1

Label: Certain

Reason: The days of the week always follow the same order. Monday always comes after Sunday.

Probability Label
1 Certain

(ii) It will snow in Mumbai in July.

Ranking: 0

Label: Impossible

Reason: Mumbai has a tropical coastal climate. July is the monsoon season, and snowfall does not occur there.

Probability Label
0 Impossible

(iii) An elephant will walk through your classroom today.

Ranking: 0

Label: Impossible

Reason: Under normal circumstances, an elephant cannot enter a classroom. Therefore, the event is considered impossible.

Probability Label
0 Impossible

(iv) You will greet at least one friend at school tomorrow.

Ranking: 0.8 (approximately)

Label: More Likely

Reason: Students usually meet and greet their friends at school. Although it is not guaranteed, it is very likely to happen.

Probability Label
0.8 More Likely
